Peak Tournaments

2019-2021

       Peak Tournaments is an organization that specializes in online broadcast and event production revolving around the Super Smash Brothers Ultimate space. Our events catered to thousands of people and consisted of online weeklies, online majors, monthlies, and more.

I founded this organization and led a team of ≈10 people in creating graphics, event planning, broadcast, and content. Our tournaments were hosted on Smash.gg (now start.gg). Our weekly tournaments occurred every Friday and in it’s peak averaged ≈150+ people. We produced and broadcasted these events to twitch.
